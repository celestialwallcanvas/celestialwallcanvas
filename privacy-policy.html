Privacy Policy — CelestialWallCanvas

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Prana Creations Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") operating as CelestialWallCanvas handles information in connection with our online wall art business. We sell digital downloads and physical canvas prints through multiple online marketplaces including Etsy (etsy.com/shop/CelestialWallCanvas) and Amazon Merch on Demand. We also operate a Pinterest business account to promote our products. Our products are produced and fulfilled by third-party print-on-demand partners including Gelato and Printseekers.

No personal information is collected directly by us through any app or website we operate. Any personal information associated with purchases is collected and processed exclusively by the marketplace platforms and our print fulfilment partners under their own privacy policies.
